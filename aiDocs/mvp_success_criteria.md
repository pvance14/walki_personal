# Walki MVP Success Criteria

## Purpose
Define explicit success criteria for the web MVP based on the validation goals in `aiDocs/context.md` and `aiDocs/mvp.md`.

## Primary Validation Criteria
- Persona appeal: at least 70% of test participants report that at least one persona feels motivating or memorable.
- Quiz effectiveness: at least 70% report that top persona results feel accurate or mostly accurate.
- Concept clarity: at least 80% can explain Walki's value proposition within 30 seconds after using the demo.
- Emotional response: at least 60% report positive emotional reaction (motivated, amused, or encouraged) to persona messages.
- Shareability intent: at least 40% indicate willingness to share results/messages or invite others.

## Secondary Criteria
- Streak visualization appeal: at least 60% rate streak/calendar as useful or engaging.
- Persona mix controls: at least 50% notice or use persona adjustment controls.
- Privacy messaging resonance: at least 70% understand that data is not sold/shared in MVP framing.

## Non-Success Signals (Watchlist)
- Users describe personas as repetitive, generic, or unclear.
- Users fail to understand quiz-to-persona personalization.
- Users drop out before reaching the interactive demo.

## Acceptance Rule
MVP is considered validated when at least 4/5 primary validation criteria meet thresholds.
