# Go / No-Go Decision Framework

**Date:** March 10, 2026

## Decision Inputs
- Persona appeal threshold performance
- Quiz accuracy perception performance
- Concept clarity performance
- Emotional response and shareability indicators
- Waitlist and qualitative feedback signal quality

## Recommendation Logic
- Go: at least 4 of 5 primary validation criteria meet thresholds.
- Conditional Go: 3 of 5 meet thresholds with clear, fixable issues and strong qualitative pull.
- No-Go: fewer than 3 of 5 meet thresholds or concept confusion remains high.

## Required Evidence Package
- Structured session summaries
- Triage report by severity and validation-goal impact
- Clear scope proposal for the next build stage
